How It All Began

Founded in 2023, CafeLove began as a small dream to create a space where coffee lovers could gather, connect, and enjoy exceptional brews in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Our founder, Sarah, spent years traveling the world to learn about different coffee cultures and brewing techniques. She brought this knowledge back to create CafeLove - a place where quality coffee and community come together.

Today, we continue to honor that original vision by sourcing the finest beans, training skilled baristas, and maintaining a welcoming space for all coffee enthusiasts.
